Women in Music History

Why is it important to study women composers, especially women composers of color?

It is important to study these composers because the vast majority of the musical canon and those who we study are white men from Europe. While there is plenty of good to study from those composers, we are missing out on a PLETHORA of music from composers who had different viewpoints, different styles, and different motivations. By bringing these composers into our classrooms, we can show just how wide the canon really is and bring role models to people who are not just white dudes.
